Individual encounters

Reserve
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Format: online
  • Personalized one-to-one sessions
  • First encounter is free and without commitment

What are they?

Individual encounters are one-to-one spaces for musicians and artists who feel that something is blocked in their relationship with music or in their creative process.

This is not a traditional lesson and not technical correction. It is a space for listening and accompaniment, starting from what you are experiencing today.

Topics we may work on

  1. Understanding where discomfort in making music comes from.
  2. Practical ways to bring play and enjoyment back into practice.
  3. Your personal project as a musician.
  4. Organization and orientation of a musical career.

What are they?

Listening to others, recognizing common difficulties, and discovering that we are often not alone in what we feel.
