Individual sessions

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Format: online
  • Personal one-to-one sessions
  • A first meeting to get to know each other

What are they?

Individual sessions are one-to-one spaces for musicians who feel that something in their relationship with music or in their creative process does not quite settle.

This is not a traditional lesson, nor a space for technical correction. It is a space for listening and accompaniment, starting from what you are experiencing today.

Topics we may work on

  1. Understanding where discomfort in making music comes from.
  2. Practical ways to bring play and ease back into instrumental practice.
  3. Your personal project as a musician.
  4. Orientation and organization of a musical career.

Group sessions

  • Format: online
  • Small groups
  • Processes over several weeks
  • Specific dates throughout the year

What are they?

Group sessions offer a shared space for musicians, where individual experiences are enriched through listening and exchange.

Hearing others, recognizing common difficulties, and discovering that many of these experiences are shared more widely than we might think.
